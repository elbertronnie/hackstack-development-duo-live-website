JoSSA Counselor
How you can use this website:
  1. Firstly we recommend you to watch the videos we have provided in home page, they will give you brief idea about JoSAA.
  2. If you still have doubts after watching videos check out the Blogs section.
  3. You will be notified whenever there is any update from JoSAA in notification section.
  4. We have a feature to check which branch and college you will get according to your rank in JEE-mains/advanced.
  5. For IIT's your JEE-Advanced rank is considered, and for NIT's,IIIT's, IIEST and Other-GFTI's your JEE-mains rank is considered.
  6. Choose whether you want to check your rank for IIT's or NIT+IIIt's
  7. You will be asked to enter your gender,branch and your rank.
  8. Website will give you list of colleges in which you can get your desired branch.
  9. You can sort list of college according to NIRF ranking,JoSAA cutoffs and placement statistics
  10. By clicking on any college from the list you will be directed to the college info page where you will get a informative video and essential information about that college
  11. We hope this will help you to decide which college to choose
